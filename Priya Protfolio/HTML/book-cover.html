Book Cover Redesign

This is a college project where I redesigned the front and back cover of the novel All the Light We Cannot See using Adobe Photoshop. The story is set during World War II and follows a blind French girl and a German boy whose lives connect in unexpected ways. My design shows a stormy sky, an old city, and the sea to reflect the book’s mood and themes of war and hope. I used a classic serif font and cool colors like blue and grey to match the emotional tone of the story. The back cover includes author reviews, a barcode, and an ISBN to make it look like a real published book.
