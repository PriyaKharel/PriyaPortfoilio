Resturant Menu

This restaurant menu design is a vibrant tribute to Nepal's rich culinary and cultural heritage. Inspired by the bold colors of the Nepali flag, the palette evokes a sense of national pride while complementing the warmth of traditional dishes like momo, selroti, and sukuti. A unique folding structure adds a playful, interactive element that invites curiosity and enhances the dining experience. To elevate the branding, a striking foil finish at the back highlights the restaurant’s name, adding a touch of elegance and sophistication rooted in authenticity.
