Box Packaging

This box packaging design for Natural Aurora’s Rosemary Tea highlights a clean, calming aesthetic that reflects the brand’s natural and wholesome identity. Inspired by the purity of herbal ingredients, the layout emphasizes clarity and wellness, while details like nutritional facts, preparation steps, and health benefits enhance functionality. The use of soft earthy tones complements the tea’s soothing nature, creating an inviting and trustworthy appeal on the shelf.
