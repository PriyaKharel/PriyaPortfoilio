Vinyl Album Design

For my college project, I designed a vinyl album for the East West Poets, titled Songs From An Eastern Western. The concept included a tracklist for Side A and B, featuring songs like “I Will Rise In Love” and “Jazz.” I incorporated the band’s logo into a cohesive design system and created professional mockups of the vinyl sleeve, record, and promotional materials, capturing the group’s unique fusion of Eastern and Western influences.
